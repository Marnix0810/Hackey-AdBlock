What is Marnix 0810 Hackey AdBlock?

HackeyBlock (the full name is Marnix 0810 Hackey AdBlock) is an adblocker which uses the hosts-file to block online advertisings and certain malicious sites, this works almost on every site, but maybe if it doesn't the site you visit is a little shady? I hope you enjoy a faster web without online advertizing or malicious content!
